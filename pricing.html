Questions?

Do these work with iPhone and Android?

Yes. It works with all iPhones (XR and newer) and almost all Androids automatically. For older phones, we include a QR code on the back.

Is there a monthly fee?

No. When you buy a card, the software is free for life for standard features. We only charge for the physical card production.

How do I design my card?

Click "Design & Order". You will be asked to upload your logo. Our team creates a digital proof for your approval before we print.
